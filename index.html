About

A litte bit about us.

  • 2012-2013

    Humble Beginnings

    We started off as sales associates in a brick-and-mortar fashion storefront. It's during one of our lunch breaks that we hatched the idea of opening an online store dedicated to high-end fashion.

  • August 2013

    Raising Our Brain Child

    We started re-selling different fashion products on Shopify. At first, we seemed to be going nowhere, but little did we know we had something going for ourselves!

  • May 2016

    The Big Leagues

    We gained our first contract with Louboutin and decided to move forward with our own professional website!

  • 2015 -

    More To Come

    Our team is constantly thinking of new ways to make our site a more enjoyable experience for our customers. Stay tuned for more updates!
